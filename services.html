Clinical Decision Support for Diabetes Management


Challenge: Improve clinical decision-making for patients with diabetes by providing personalized treatment plans based on real-time patient data.
Solution: We developed an AI-powered clinical decision support system that monitors blood glucose levels, patient history, and lifestyle factors. The system analyzes data from continuous glucose monitors (CGMs) and recommends insulin dosages, dietary changes, and medication adjustments.
Outcome: The system improved glycemic control in 85% of patients by providing personalized treatment plans, reducing the risk of complications by 30%, and decreasing the need for manual interventions by healthcare providers.
